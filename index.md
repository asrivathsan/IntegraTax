# **Welcome to IntegraTax**

**IntegraTax** is a user-friendly software for clustering DNA sequences and visualising dendrograms to support species delimitation and taxonomic research.

IntegraTax provides two core functions:

**1.** **Clustering** - Groups your sequences based on pairwise distances (single linkage/Objective clustering
